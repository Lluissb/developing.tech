About Me

Hey! I'm Lluís Saura. I'm studying a Bachelor Degree in Political Sciences in the University of Valencia, Spain. However, my true passion is the web, so coding amazing websites and showing all I can do is my life's path career.


I started developing a few years ago. Back then, I used pre-designed platforms such as Wix or Weebly. Although I liked it that way, I figured out I wanted to express myself at a thousand per cent, so I decided to try coding by myself. In that moment, I decided to self-teach myself, starting with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Later on, when I had a basic knowledge, I started with jQuery, SCSS, and others.


After a few months of learning, debugging, coding, and getting to know this world, I made the decision to become a Front-End Web Developer.
